About NinjaCart

How It Works

NinjaCart is a platform that connects local farmers, grocery suppliers, and consumers. Users can browse a wide range of products categorized into vegetables, fruits, and groceries. Simply select the items you want to buy and add them to your cart.

How Products Are Bought

After adding items to your cart, proceed to checkout. You will be shown a summary of your order and total price. Once you click "Pay Now", the order is processed and confirmed. A delivery executive picks up the items from verified sellers and delivers them to your doorstep.

Product Certification

All our vendors are registered and vetted through a verification process. Products are sourced directly from certified farms and authorized distributors. Quality checks are conducted to ensure freshness, cleanliness, and proper packaging before delivery.
